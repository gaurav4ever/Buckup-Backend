what is Body Fat percentage? How do I calculate it?
Your body fat percentage (BFP) is your total mass of fat divided by total bodymass; body fat includes essentialbody fat and storage body fat. It is a more reliable method than Body Mass Index (BMI) since it relies on relative adiposity( fat content) and not your height and weight.
